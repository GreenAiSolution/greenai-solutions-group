04The 80/20 Rule

Phenomenal salespeople listen 80% of the time and talk 20%. Your job in this meeting isn't to recite the pitch — it's to get Collin talking about his business, his pain points, and his goals, then show him exactly how GreenAI solves that specific thing. The script above is ammunition, not a monologue.

05Questions to Ask Collin

Open the meeting — get him talking

1. "Tell me about your business — what's working right now and what's frustrating you?"
2. "What have you already tried for marketing — social media, ads, a website? What happened with it?"
3. "Who's your ideal customer, and how do they usually find you today?"

Qualify the opportunity

4. "What's your current monthly revenue?" (plug into the ROI calculator live)
5. "How many followers do you have across all platforms right now?"
6. "Do you have any budget set aside for ads currently?"
7. "Who's handling your marketing now — you, an employee, another agency, nobody?"

Uncover the real objection before he says it

8. "If money weren't a factor, what would you want your marketing to look like in 90 days?"
9. "What's stopped you from doing this already?"
10. "What would have to be true for you to feel confident saying yes today?"

Close

11. "Does this make sense for where your business is right now?"
12. "Want to go ahead and get you onboarded today so we can start this week?"
