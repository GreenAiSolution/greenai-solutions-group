Common Questions

Everything You're Wondering About

Straight answers to the questions we hear most often.

What exactly gets built each month?
It depends entirely on your business needs. In your first month, we identify the single highest-impact piece of software for your business and build it completely — typically a client portal, a custom CRM, a booking system, or an internal dashboard. In subsequent months, we iterate on existing builds and add new capabilities. Some clients build 2–3 new tools per month; others go deep on evolving one core system. You drive the roadmap.
How long does it take to go from idea to live software?
Most builds go live the same day or within 24–48 hours of our session. A standard booking system or client portal takes 4–8 hours to build and deploy. More complex applications with advanced integrations may take 2–3 days. Compare that to 3–6 months with a traditional developer — and you'll understand why we call this a factory.
Do I need any technical knowledge at all?
Absolutely not. You don't need to know what a database is, what an API does, or what "full-stack" means. You talk to us in plain English about your business problem and what you want to accomplish. We translate that into working software. Your only job is to describe your vision clearly — and that's something every business owner already knows how to do.
Who owns the software that gets built?
You own it entirely. The software we build for you is your intellectual property. If you ever cancel, you keep everything that was built — all the code, all the data, all the deployments. We'll provide you with a complete handoff package. You're never locked in or held hostage by us owning your tools.
How is this different from Bubble, Webflow, or other no-code tools?
No-code tools require you to do the building yourself — you learn the platform, drag and drop, and wrestle with limitations. GreenFit AI Creator is done-for-you. We do all the work. There are also no platform limitations — because we're writing real code with AI, there is no ceiling on what we can build. You're not constrained by what Bubble or Webflow decided to support.
What if I want changes after something is built?
That's exactly what the monthly subscription is for. Changes, improvements, expansions, and new features are all handled in your monthly iteration session — and for urgent fixes or small tweaks, direct access to Jaden means you can request changes any time. Your software is never "frozen" — it always moves forward.
How many clients does GreenFit AI Creator accept?
We keep the roster intentionally small to maintain the quality and attention that makes this service worth $1,400/month. Every client gets direct access to Jaden — and that means we can only take on a limited number of clients at once. Every application is reviewed personally and we only accept clients we're certain we can deliver extraordinary results for.
Is there a setup fee or minimum commitment?
No setup fee. No long-term contract. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. We earn your continued business by delivering continuous value every single month — not by locking you in. If it's not working, you walk away with everything we built for you and owe nothing more.
