Outreach & Sales

The Client Acquisition Machine

Paid ads bring leads. This outreach system converts them — and generates leads independently at zero cost.

01

The Instagram DM Blitz

Send 20–30 DMs per day to local businesses in your target niches. Don't pitch immediately — open with a genuine observation about their business. "Came across your work — your detailing shots are clean but I notice you're not posting daily. Are you getting inbound from IG right now?" Get the conversation going before you sell. Convert to a demo call.

02

The Pre-Built Demo Close

Before every sales call, build a custom 30-day content calendar, sample posts, and a mock website for their specific business. Walk in with the deliverable already done. When they see their business in the GreenAI system before signing, the close rate jumps to 70%+. This is your secret weapon — use it every time.

03

Cold Email Sequences

Find business owner emails via Google Business, their website contact pages, or LinkedIn. Send a 3-email sequence over 7 days: Email 1 = problem (their current social media is missing money). Email 2 = proof (a competitor's results). Email 3 = offer (free demo, no pitch). Keep every email under 150 words. Curiosity over information.

04

Google Maps Prospecting

Open Google Maps, search "car detailer Gilbert AZ" (or any niche + city). Every business without a website or with a weak Instagram is a hot lead. Search 5 niches × 10 cities = 50 prospects per hour. Add them to your outreach spreadsheet and work the list systematically every morning.

05

In-Person Drive-By Visits

Show up at barbershops, detailers, and restaurants during slow hours (Tuesday-Thursday, 10AM–2PM). Don't sell — introduce yourself, say you're a local AI marketing agency and you built something for their shop. Pull out your laptop or iPad and show the demo you built for them. Book a follow-up on the spot. In-person demos convert at 60–80%.

06

Facebook Group Marketing

Join 10–20 local business Facebook Groups ("Gilbert AZ Business Network," "East Valley Entrepreneurs," "Chandler Small Business Owners"). Post valuable content weekly — tips, client wins, free resources. When members engage, DM them. Do not post your services directly — let value convert them. Position yourself as the expert, not the salesperson.

Referral Engine

Build a Referral Machine

One happy detailer knows five other detailers. One barbershop owner knows every other shop owner in the city. Referrals are your lowest-cost acquisition channel.

🤝

Client Referral Program

Give every active client a referral link. For every new client they send you that signs, give them one free month of service. A $700 client sending you a $1,200 client costs you $700 and earns you $1,200 recurring. That math prints money.

🏗️

Strategic Partnerships

Partner with: business accountants (their clients need marketing), business attorneys (new LLCs need websites), commercial real estate agents (new tenants need marketing). Offer 10% referral commission.

🎓

Chamber of Commerce

Join the Gilbert, Chandler, and Mesa Chambers of Commerce. Attend monthly mixers. Sponsor one event per quarter. One speaking slot at a chamber event can generate 5–8 leads in a single night.

📦

White-Label Partnerships

Approach local web designers and graphic designers — offer to white-label your social media services under their brand. They sell it to their clients, you execute, they take 20%. Passive client pipeline with no outreach.

⭐

Google Review Campaign

Ask every client for a Google review after month 1. Offer a small bonus (extra posts, a free story set) for leaving one. 20 five-star reviews makes you the #1 social media agency in Gilbert on Google Maps — and that's free inbound forever.

🎙️

Local Podcast Appearances

Pitch yourself as a guest on Phoenix-area business podcasts and YouTube channels. "AI Marketing for Local Businesses in 2026" is a hot topic. One episode = 500–2,000 targeted listeners who already trust the host. Three episodes per quarter minimum.

90-Day Plan

Week-by-Week Execution

Every week has a primary objective. Stick to the sequence — foundations before growth, growth before scale.

Week 1–2 — Foundation

Get the Machine Ready

  • Finalize all GreenAI social media profiles — IG, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube. All bios link to greenaidigital.com
  • Film 10 pieces of content in one sitting (batching): intros, demos, tips, process videos
  • Set up CRM (even a Google Sheet): Name, niche, contact, status, follow-up date
  • Schedule first 14 days of posts using Buffer or Later
  • Build your first 5 pre-demo packages (1 for each niche: detailing, barbershop, restaurant, fitness, landscaping)
  • Set up Meta Business Manager and Google Ads accounts — even if you don't run ads yet
Week 3–4 — First Clients

Close the First 3 Paying Clients

  • Send 20 Instagram DMs per day — all niche-specific, all personalized
  • Do 5 in-person drop-ins per week to local businesses
  • Run first Meta ad: $15/day, free demo offer, local targeting
  • Present at least 3 pre-built demos
  • Onboard first client within 48 hours of signing — immediate delivery builds trust
  • Ask first client for a video testimonial at end of week 4
Week 5–6 — Proof

Generate Case Studies & Scale Outreach

  • Capture first client results: follower growth, reach, booking increases
  • Create before/after content showing client wins — post on all channels
  • Increase DM outreach to 30/day
  • Join 5 local Facebook business groups — post value content, no pitching
  • Aim for 7 total clients by end of week 6
  • Launch Google Local Service Ads — $10/day budget
Week 7–8 — Referral Engine

Turn Clients Into a Sales Team

  • Launch formal referral program to all active clients
  • Request Google reviews from every client
  • Pitch 3 strategic partnership conversations (accountant, attorney, web designer)
  • Attend one Chamber of Commerce event
  • Create a "GreenAI Results" highlight reel video — post everywhere
  • Target: 12 active clients by end of week 8
Week 9–10 — Scale

Double Down on What's Working

  • Identify top 2 lead sources and pour resources there
  • Increase ad spend to $50/day on winning Meta creatives
  • Launch TikTok Spark Ads on top-performing organic video
  • Pitch 2 white-label partnership deals
  • Record first YouTube long-form video
  • Target: 16 active clients
Week 11–12 — Optimize & Lock In

Hit 20 Clients. Build the Retention System.

  • Monthly strategy calls scheduled with every Growth/Dominator client
  • Build a client dashboard showing each account's performance monthly
  • Upsell Starter clients to Growth — show them results data
  • Close remaining clients needed to hit 20 total
  • Document all processes for eventual team hire
  • Plan Q4 expansion: hire first VA or contractor to handle content output
