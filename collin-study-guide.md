3. After generating, edits to this field instantly re-render the canvas.

### Body Text + CTA Dropdown (150 chars)
Body text renders smaller below the headline. CTA dropdown adds a colored button on the canvas.

**Steps:**
1. Type body copy: "Premium ceramic coating. Same-day booking. Gilbert's #1 rated detailer."
2. Select **"Book Now"** from CTA dropdown — gradient button appears on canvas.
3. Change to **"DM Us"** and watch it swap instantly.

---

### Hashtags + One-Click Suggestions
Type manually or click the pre-generated suggestion chips. Chips add that hashtag to the field automatically. Suggestions change per platform.

**Steps:**
1. Show suggestion chips — click a few and watch them auto-add to the field.
2. Hashtags render in accent color at the bottom of the canvas.
3. Switch platforms (Instagram → TikTok) — suggestions change to #fyp, #foryou, etc.

---

**Steps:**

3. Set the **Brand Handle** field to Collin's username — it renders at the bottom of every post.

## 13 — Content Studio — Video Recording & Download
**Location:** Content Studio → Video Settings panel (shows when Reel/Video content type is selected)

**The most impressive feature.** Select a video content type, choose animation settings, record a fully animated video — all in the browser, no software needed.

**Steps:**
1. Select **Instagram → Reel** (or TikTok → Video).
2. The **Video Settings panel** appears — show Animation Style dropdown: Slide In, Fade, Zoom In, Typewriter, Bounce.
3. Set duration to **10 seconds**. Select **Floating Dots** for particles.
4. Hit **Generate Content** first to render the base frame.
5. Click **Download Video** — red **REC** indicator appears with progress bar.
6. Watch the animation play live on the canvas.
7. When recording finishes, a **.webm video file downloads** automatically. Open and play for Collin.

**Animation Styles:**

| Animation | Effect | Best Use |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Slide In | Text slides in from the right | Bold announcements, launches |
| Fade | Text fades in gently | Luxury, premium, calm brands |
| Zoom In | Text scales up from center | Energetic, call to action heavy |
| Typewriter | Text types out character by character | Tech, educational, AI brands |
| Bounce | Text pulses with a heartbeat effect | Fitness, events, urgent offers |

**Talking Points:**
- "This creates a full animated video — no Canva, no Adobe Premiere, no external software. Right in the browser."
- "TikTok and Reels favor video content 3-5x over static images. This lets us produce video at scale."
- "The .webm file can be uploaded directly to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook."
- "We can generate 10 different animated posts for Collin's business in about 20 minutes."
