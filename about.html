The Opportunity

Why AI is the Biggest Shift
in Real Estate in 30 Years

The last major disruption in real estate was the internet — portals like Zillow and Realtor.com changed how buyers search, and agents who adapted thrived. The agents who ignored it fell behind.

AI is that moment again. Except the gap between early adopters and late adopters is going to be far more dramatic — because AI doesn't just change how clients find properties, it changes every single function of an agent's business simultaneously.

The agents using AI are writing better listings faster, following up with every lead without lifting a finger, walking into every appointment more prepared, and spending more of their time on high-value human connection. The agents ignoring AI are competing against them.

The question isn't whether to adopt AI. The question is how soon, and how well.
