Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use your services?

Absolutely not. We handle all the technical setup, integration, and ongoing management. You'll use simple, intuitive tools — most of which work inside your existing CRM or email platform. If you can send a text message, you can use our AI tools.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see measurable improvement in lead response rates and listing engagement within the first 30 days. Meaningful pipeline growth typically shows up in weeks 6"12. We track everything from day one so you can see exactly what's working.

Will the AI sound like me?

Yes — this is a priority for us. During onboarding we collect writing samples, learn your tone, and train the AI to communicate in your voice. Clients and leads won't know an AI was involved. You review and approve communication flows before they go live.

What CRMs and tools do you integrate with?

We integrate with the major real estate platforms including Follow Up Boss, KVCore, LionDesk, Chime, Salesforce, HubSpot, and most MLS systems. If you're using something else, reach out — our setup team can almost certainly make it work.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All GreenAI plans are month-to-month. We earn your business every month through results, not contracts. Cancel anytime — no questions asked.

Do you work with agents outside of Arizona?

Yes — while we're based in Gilbert, AZ, we work with agents across the country. Our services are fully remote and everything is managed virtually. We currently have clients in Arizona, California, Texas, Florida, and Colorado.
