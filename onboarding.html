GreenAI Solutions — Service Agreement Summary 1. Services
GreenAI Solutions agrees to provide the selected social media management, advertising, and/or website development services as outlined in the chosen package. Specific deliverables will be confirmed in a separate scope document within 3 business days of signing.

2. Payment Terms
Monthly retainer fees are due on the 1st of each billing month. A valid payment method must be on file. Late payments (beyond 7 days) may result in a pause in services. Website projects require a 50% deposit before work begins.

3. No Long-Term Contract
Services are provided on a month-to-month basis. Either party may cancel with 14 days written notice. No cancellation fees apply.

4. Content Approval
Client maintains final approval on all content based on the approval preference selected. GreenAI is not liable for delays caused by late client approvals. Autopilot clients grant GreenAI posting authority.

5. Account Access & Security
Client grants GreenAI manager-level access to all managed platforms. GreenAI will never request account passwords — access is granted via official platform admin tools only. Client retains full ownership of all accounts.

6. Ad Spend
Ad budgets are billed directly to the client's ad accounts. GreenAI management fees are separate from ad spend. GreenAI is not responsible for platform policy violations outside our control.

7. Results & Guarantees
GreenAI guarantees measurable improvement within 30 days or will work the following month at no charge. Specific guarantees are contingent on client providing timely access, approvals, and cooperation.

8. Intellectual Property
All content created by GreenAI for the client becomes the client's property upon full payment. GreenAI may use anonymized performance data and non-identifiable work samples in its portfolio unless the client opts out in writing.

9. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep proprietary information confidential. GreenAI will not share client business data, strategy, or financials with third parties.
