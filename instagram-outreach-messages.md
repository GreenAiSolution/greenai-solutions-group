## Usage Notes

- **Personalize the opener** — reference something specific from their profile (a recent post, their location, their niche). Generic DMs get ignored.
- **One message at a time** — send the hook, wait for a reply before adding more context.
- **Follow up once** — if no reply after 3–5 days, send a single short follow-up ("Hey [Name] — still open to that quick call if timing was just off!"). Then move on.
- **Lead with curiosity, not a pitch** — the goal of the first DM is a reply, not a sale.
- **Best sending times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am or 6–8pm local time.
