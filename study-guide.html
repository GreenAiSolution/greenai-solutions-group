13 Content Studio

Content Studio — Video Recording & Download

The most impressive feature. Select a video content type (Reel, TikTok Video, etc.), choose animation settings, and record a fully animated video — all in the browser, no software needed.

🎬
Video Settings + MediaRecorder
Content Studio → Video Settings panel (shows when Reel/Video selected)

When you select Reel (Instagram/Facebook) or Video (TikTok), the Video Options panel appears with Animation Style, Duration, and Background Particles. The Download Video button becomes active after generating. Clicking it records the animated canvas and downloads a .webm video file.

1
Select Instagram → Reel from the content types (or TikTok → Video).
2
The Video Settings panel appears — show Collin the Animation Style dropdown: Slide In, Fade, Zoom In, Typewriter, Bounce.
3
Set duration to 10 seconds. Select Floating Dots for particles.
4
Hit Generate Content first to render the base frame.
5
Click Download Video — the red REC indicator appears. The progress bar tracks duration.
6
Watch the animation play live on the canvas — text slides/zooms/bounces, particles float, a scanline sweeps across.
7
When recording finishes, a .webm video file downloads automatically. Open it and play for Collin.
